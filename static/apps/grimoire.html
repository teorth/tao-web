Lean Grimoire

A proof-crafting game. Combine the statements you already hold — the potions 🧪, each a proof hA : A — with the recipes (Lean-named laws) to brew new statements, until you craft the goal. A live Lean proof assembles itself in the spellbook as you play. A rebuilt, Lean-flavoured descendant of the QED game.

    How to play
    
Select one or more potions on your bench; the recipes light up with the statements they
      let you brew from that selection. Click a brew to add it to your bench — and watch the corresponding
      have appear in the spellbook. Craft the goal to finish the proof (the
      sorry turns into the final step). Undo steps back; names like
      hAB are auto-chosen but the objects and their types are kept typographically
      distinct. The Lean produced is deliberately plain, step-by-step pseudo-Lean — the point is to
      see the proof being built, not to write the slickest tactic.
