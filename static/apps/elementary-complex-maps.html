Elementary complex maps

See how a complex function w = f(z) distorts the plane. Draw a curve with the mouse in the left panel (the domain) and its image under f appears on the right. Move the cursor to preview a point z and its image f(z).

A modern JavaScript + Canvas port of a 2002 Java applet by Terence Tao (originally ComplexMap.java / ComplexGrid.java).
