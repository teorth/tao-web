Harmonic-analysis exponent calculator

Work out the "exponent numerology" of the estimates that come up constantly in harmonic analysis and PDE — Hölder, Sobolev, Young, fractional integration, the Strichartz estimates, the restriction/extension conjectures, interpolation, and scaling. Pick an estimate, fill in what you know, and solve for the unknown. Entries are rational (3/2, -3, 1/0 for ∞) and may be symbolic (d, 2n+1, (a+b)/c; use nn for n², and \eps for multi-letter names).

A modern JavaScript port of a 1999 Java applet by Terence Tao (originally Calculator.java and friends).
